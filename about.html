My Brain


My brain works in wierd ways. I know I am not alone in this, but besides my lover, it feels i am.
You may be asking how my brain is wierd. To answer you, i have (you guessed it) another list, in no particular order of wierd-ness.

  • I seem to forget that im supposed to clean things up after spending 30 minutes in the kitchen or eating in my room.
    I also seem to forget within the 3 seconds I take cleaning that moving a dirty plate from my desk to on top of another plate is not "cleaning".
  • When i lost anything, I never search under anything, only on top. If i set my coat down and im looking for my wallet, I will look on top of my jacket, then all over the house for an hour before I think to check the jacket's pockets.
  • My brain justifies procrastination as "self-care" and/or "rewards for my hard work", even when it knows i did about two practice problems to study for that chemistry test.
This and more, are all things i am trying actively to reduce the commonality of. However, these have been traits of mine for as long as I can remember. So, it'll be hard.
